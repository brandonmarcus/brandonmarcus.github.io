Cloud Architect

Introduction

Brandon has extensive experience leading teams, developing IT strategies and has core expertise in implementing public cloud infrastructures in AWS, Azure and Office 365.

Professional Profile

Brandon is a results oriented business and information technology planner and facilitator; who has spent over 20 years helping many highly respected companies to design and implement large-scale application and infrastructure solutions. During that time, he has helped companies build their cloud strategies, product managed new service offerings, lead the infrastructure design for company acquisitions and built large scale infrastructures.

Brandon has extensive experience leading teams and core expertise in implementing cloud infrastructure in AWS, Azure and Office 365. His experience has led him to learn architecture and operations while at Microsoft Corporation. As an Architecture Consultant at Microsoft, Brandon’s expertise was in Microsoft’s collaboration stack, directory technologies and security.
